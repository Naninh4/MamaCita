to learn more

Useful links to sites and tools to assist you in citations and management of bibliographic references!


ABNT

Brazilian Association of Technical Standards. It has documents with the standards to be followed in citations. The correct way to acknowledge the mentioned voices is regulated by this organization, which standardizes the ways of presenting the work of others. The current standard is NBR 10520 "Information and Documentation - Citations in Documents - Presentation", from August 2002.

BIBLIOGRAPHIC
REFERENCES

Bibliographic references construction site: its model can follow different standards, such as ABNT and APA. The use becomes easy and practical, since it is enough to provide some data about the work that it will be well recognized by the sources provided in the bibliographic reference.

MORE

Tool for production and organization of references (also provides the "author, date" model) for use in the text and for construction of bibliographic references. It covers various types of documents such as: books, encyclopedias, dissertations, theses, etc.

ZOTERO

Zotero is a platform with free open source resources. It supports over 10,000 reference styles and does not require installation to be used. It is a site for sharing, collecting, citing, and organizing research.
