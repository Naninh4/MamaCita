Welcome to MamaCita!

Your new academic ally. Science voice management and the importance of clearly explained scientific works for you.

A non-profit project aimed at assisting students (especially those who are just starting out in the scientific field) in academic practices for the advancement of science.

Referring to the expertise of specialists in your project's field, for example, lends greater credibility to the academic work and instills confidence that the study conducted has significant societal impact.

The content of the site covers the needs and practices aimed at by the beginner writer, also considering possible challenges and doubts in academic text writing and literature management.

Let's talk a little about the importance of citing?

Feak and Swales (2009, cited in MOTTA-ROTH; HENDGES, 2010) bring the possibility of comparing referencing previous literature to dwarves standing on the shoulders of giants, considering the opportunity to "see further" from previous scientific works. Furthermore, Motta-Roth and Hendges (2010) also point out that referencing can be understood as a fundamental matter in ethical academic work, especially if we consider how reliable it is to rely on previous scientific studies.

How did MamaCita come about?

The idea for MamaCita arose from the production of an Integrative Project by students of the Technical Course in Computer Science Integrated with High School at IFRN. Its theme, focused on academic and informational content to assist beginner writers, was presented to us in Scientific Initiation groups, where we studied linguistics and science voice management. Combining the useful with the pleasant, MamaCita emerges as an academic ally. Its name refers to the first contact, the knowledge conveyed in a welcoming manner, and the mechanism of language in the act of citing. After all, MamaCita is about education and science.
